About

The Fibonacci FM Explorer is a browser-based instrument built on Csound 7 WebAudio. It translates the mathematics of the Fibonacci sequence and golden ratio into sound through John Chowning's FM synthesis — connecting number theory, nature, and music in a single interactive experience.

Designed as both a creative tool and a pedagogical instrument, it invites musicians, students, and curious minds to hear what mathematical beauty sounds like.

Dr. Richard Boulanger

Berklee College of Music
