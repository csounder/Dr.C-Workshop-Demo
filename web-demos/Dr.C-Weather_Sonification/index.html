Features

🎹

6-Layer Musical System

Chords, arpeggios, walking bass, evolving drone, melody, and counter-melody — all responding to weather in real-time.

🌍

17 Global Cities

From Tokyo to Reykjavik, each location has unique timbral characteristics and musical keys. Use GPS for your exact location.

🎼

7 Modal Scales

Choose from Ionian, Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, Aeolian, or Locrian modes for different emotional characters.

🎨

4 Orchestral Palettes

Warm Pads, Breathy FM, Crystal Bells, or Textural Granular — each with 20+ unique Csound instruments.

🌊

Phrase Mode

Intelligent layer fading creates dynamic arrangements that evolve over time, from silence to full ensemble.

📊

6 Visualizations

Spectrum analyzer, waterfall FFT, sonogram, particle system, chord diagrams, and 24-hour weather graphs.

🎚️

Advanced Controls

Independent volume, speed, and busyness controls for each layer. Transpose, tempo, and timbre adjustments.

🔄

Auto Location Change

Automatically cycle through cities to explore global weather patterns and discover unique sonic landscapes.

🧠

Markov Chain Harmony

Chord progressions generated using weighted Markov chains for natural, non-repetitive musical flow.

How It Works

The system fetches real-time weather data from the Open-Meteo API and maps atmospheric parameters to musical elements. Temperature affects melody range, wind controls rhythm and activity, humidity shapes reverb and articulation, pressure determines bass depth, and cloud cover influences filter brightness.
